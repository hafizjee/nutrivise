How This App Works

Health Nutrition Assistant is designed to help users understand the possible nutritional causes behind their health issues — such as hair fall, weak bones, poor eyesight, or skin problems — and suggest natural remedies.

Step-by-step Process:
  • ✅ Analyze Affected Body Part: Detect which organ or area is linked to your symptom.
  • ✅ Detect Deficiencies: Identify missing vitamins or minerals (e.g., calcium, iron, vitamin A).
  • ✅ Suggest Natural Foods: Recommend fruits, herbs, or plants rich in those nutrients.

All suggestions are based on natural remedies, not medication.
